# Brothers Creed Barbering Company Website

A premium barbershop website for Brothers Creed Barbering Company located in Belleville, NJ.

## Features

- **Responsive Design**: Works seamlessly on desktop and mobile devices
- **Premium Styling**: Elegant black and gold theme matching the barbershop's branding
- **Online Booking**: Direct integration with Square booking system
- **Service Menu**: Complete pricing and service descriptions
- **Location Info**: Address, hours, and embedded Google Maps
- **Contact Options**: Phone booking and online appointment scheduling

## Services & Pricing

- **Men's Haircut**: $40 (Full haircut & style for 18 & over)
- **Beard**: $30 (Beard grooming and hot towel - 15 minutes)
- **Youth Haircut**: $20 (Full haircut & style for 17 & under)
- **Shape-up**: $20 (Sideburn outline & Neck outline)
- **Seniors Haircut**: $30 (Full haircut & style for 65+)
